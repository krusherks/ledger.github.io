About App

Keep track of money and settle using any form of payment

Ledger helps in splitting bills between friends & family. Keep track of dues & expenses, send reminders for settlement, & in-app settlement using the UPI & wallets.

Ledger is the first UPI payment portal for peers & Merchants. We provide interoperability with all payment wallets, multiple UPI id linking, no transaction limits & instant expense tracking on the app.
