Benadryl Challenge

Warnings of Overdose Risk?

The so-called “Benadryl Challenge” went viral on TikTok in 2020, challenging teenagers to overdose on the allergy medication to elicit hallucinatory effects, prompting warnings from medical experts over the potential risk of overdose death. Call 911 immediately if you or someone you know may be suffering from an overdose.
